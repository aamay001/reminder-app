Welcome to TellMeonDate!

TellMeonDate is a reminder application. After you sign up for an account, TellMeonDate can currently send you a reminder via SMS (Text Message). Once you're logged in, all you have to do is schedule your reminder and TellMeonDate will send it to you when it's time to remind you. Click the login button below to login or, click the register button to register for an account.
